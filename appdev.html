MoodScroller: Aesthetically Pleasing Moderation

This is a prototype of an app me and a partner worked on. It is called MoodScroller, and is a more aesthetically pleasing way to stop yourself from endlessly scrolling harmful media.

An almost entirely functional prototype of the app. Do note that settings cannot save in Figma and also that changing the time you have on example apps is also not possible. If you click somewhere that isn't a link, Figma should highlight what you can click in blue.
